### 1. Add, Multiply, and Subtract  
**Description**:  
Write a program that accepts two integer inputs, **x** and **y**. First, add **x** and **y**, then multiply the sum by 3, and subtract 10 from the result.  

**Sample Output 1**:  
Enter x: 5  
Enter y: 3  
Result: 14  

**Sample Output 2**:  
Enter x: 7  
Enter y: 8  
Result: 35

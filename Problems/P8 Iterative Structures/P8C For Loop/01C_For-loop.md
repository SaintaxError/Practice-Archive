# For Loop Problems

---

## **Problem 1: Even Numbers Between 1 and N**

**Description:** Ask the user for a number `N`, and print all even numbers from 1 to `N`.

- Use a `for` loop to print all even numbers in the range.

**Sample Output:**

```
Enter a number: 10
2 4 6 8 10
```

